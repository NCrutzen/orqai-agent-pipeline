Key structural points (per D-10/D-11/D-12 — verify after paste):

   - `reject`: no-op (per D-10; the `unknown` seed routes here).
   - `manual_review`: no-op (per D-10; reserved for future).
   - `categorize_archive`: gate categorize+archive on `category.outlook_label !== null` (D-11, Q1 — skip BOTH when null). Pass `category.outlook_label!` to `categorizeEmail`. iController-delete step gated on `swarm_type === "debtor-email"` (D-12).
