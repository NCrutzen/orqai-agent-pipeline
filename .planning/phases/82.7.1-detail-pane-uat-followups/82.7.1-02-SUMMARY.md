# Phase 82.7.1 Plan 02: Row Fade-Out on pendingRemoval Summary

Approve and Submit-override now produce a 150ms in-place opacity fade-out before the row unmounts, closing E-02 silent-UX gap while preserving 82.6 D-03 (no toasts) and 82.5 D-2 (grid contract).

## What Changed

`web/app/(dashboard)/automations/[swarm]/_shell/row-list.tsx`:

1. **Local deferred-unmount Set** added: `const [_unmount, setUnmount] = useState<Set<string>>(() => new Set())`.
2. **useEffect** schedules `setTimeout(180ms)` per new entry in `pendingRemovalIds`, adding it to `_unmount` when the timer fires; timers are cleared on cleanup.
3. **Render filter swapped** from `pendingRemovalIds` to `_unmount` — rows stay in DOM during the fade.
4. **Per-row `<li>` style** gained three props (`opacity`, `transition: "opacity 150ms ease-out"`, `pointerEvents`) plus a `data-pending` attribute and pending-aware `cursor`. `gridTemplateColumns` is byte-identical (D-2 contract preserved).

D-05 consistency is automatic: Phase 82.7-04's `markPendingRemovalAndAdvance` and the Submit-override flow both already write to `pendingRemovalIds`, so both paths inherit the fade with zero special-casing in this plan.
