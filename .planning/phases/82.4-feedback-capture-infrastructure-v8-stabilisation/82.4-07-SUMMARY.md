# Phase 82.4 Plan 07: Nightly email_feedback Snapshot Cron — Summary

Inngest cron `feedback/nightly-snapshot` ships at 02:00 Europe/Amsterdam and writes a deterministic JSON dump of the last 26h of `public.email_feedback` to `email-feedback-snapshots/YYYY-MM-DD.json` in Supabase Storage. Closes FB-11 and gives V9.0 a frozen daily corpus to iterate the clusterer against without churning live data.

- **Duration:** ~6 min

## Accomplishments

- TDD RED → GREEN cycle: 7 vitest cases covering cron config, step.run sequencing, upload path shape, upload opts, empty-snapshot determinism, 26h read window, and return shape — all passing.
- Replay-safety locked: `run_id` (`crypto.randomUUID()`) and `snapshot_date` (UTC-derived date string) both resolved inside `step.run` per the Phase 65 lock; combined with `upsert:true` on the Storage upload the daily artifact is fully idempotent regardless of replay count.
- Defensive `.limit(10000)` row cap with `console.warn` breach signal — T-82.4-07-03 storage-exhaustion threat mitigated for v8.0 cardinality (~50/day expected).
- Function registered in `web/app/api/inngest/route.ts` adjacent to `stage0CoverageProbe` (cron-style group convention).
