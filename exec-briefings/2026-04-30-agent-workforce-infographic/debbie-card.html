What Debbie does

  • Wrangles the seven Handlers. When the Intent-Routing Agent surfaces a clear single intent, Debbie hands the email straight to the right specialist.
  • Splits the job when needed. An email with two intents — "send me invoice 12345 AND change my address" — gets broken into parallel jobs. Debbie spawns multiple Handlers and waits for them to finish.
  • Stitches their outputs together. Synthesises the parallel Handler results into a single iController draft so the operator gets one reply, not three.
  • Knows when to escalate. Low confidence, three+ intents, or a registry-flagged "needs orchestration" intent → Debbie pulls a human in instead of guessing.
  • Keeps cost predictable. Default 80% of inbound stays on the cheap single-shot path; only the genuinely complex emails pay for the orchestrator step.
